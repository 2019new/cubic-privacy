「指尖魔方」隐私政策

最后更新日期：2026 年 6 月 26 日

感谢您使用「指尖魔方」（以下简称“本应用”）。我们高度重视您的隐私。 本隐私政策旨在说明本应用如何处理与您相关的信息。请您在使用本应用前仔细阅读。

1. 我们不收集任何个人信息

本应用是一款单机离线的魔方益智游戏。本应用不会收集、存储、上传或共享您的任何个人信息，包括但不限于：姓名、电话号码、电子邮箱、位置信息、通讯录、相册、设备标识符等。

2. 无需注册、无需联网

本应用无需注册账号，也无需登录。本应用的核心功能（魔方还原、图案挑战）均在您的设备本地运行，无需连接互联网即可使用。

3. 本地数据

本应用可能会在您的设备本地保存少量数据，仅用于改善您的游戏体验，例如：当前对局进度、游戏设置（音效、震动开关）等。这些数据仅存储在您的设备上，不会被上传至任何服务器，也不会被我们或任何第三方读取。卸载本应用后，这些本地数据将被一并删除。

4. 第三方服务

当前版本的本应用不集成任何第三方广告、数据统计或分析服务，不会向任何第三方传输数据。若未来版本引入此类服务（例如广告），我们将更新本隐私政策并明确告知。

5. 儿童隐私

本应用适合所有年龄段用户使用。由于本应用不收集任何个人信息，因此也不会收集儿童的任何个人信息。

6. 隐私政策的变更

我们可能会不时更新本隐私政策。任何变更都会在本页面公布，并更新页首的“最后更新日期”。建议您定期查阅本页面以了解最新信息。

"FingerCube" Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 26, 2026

Thank you for using "FingerCube" (the "App"). We take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles information related to you. Please read it carefully before using the App.

1. We Do Not Collect Any Personal Information

The App is a standalone, offline Rubik's Cube puzzle game. The App does not collect, store, upload, or share any of your personal information, including but not limited to: name, phone number, email address, location, contacts, photos, or device identifiers.

2. No Registration, No Network Required

The App does not require account registration or login. Its core features (solving the cube, pattern challenges) run locally on your device and do not require an internet connection.

3. Local Data

The App may store a small amount of data locally on your device solely to improve your experience, such as current game progress and settings (sound, vibration). This data is stored only on your device, is never uploaded to any server, and is not accessed by us or any third party. Uninstalling the App removes this local data.

4. Third-Party Services

The current version of the App does not integrate any third-party advertising, analytics, or tracking services and does not transmit data to any third party. If future versions introduce such services (e.g., ads), we will update this Privacy Policy and notify you accordingly.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is suitable for users of all ages. Because the App does not collect any personal information, it does not collect any personal information from children.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
